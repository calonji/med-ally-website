Key Challenges in Medical Decision-Making

Healthcare professionals often grapple with information overload. The massive amount of data generated daily—from EHRs, lab results, and clinical studies—can be overwhelming.

  • Traditional decision-making processes are often hampered by inefficiencies and human error, which can lead to diagnostic delays, unnecessary procedures, and subpar patient outcomes.
  • Moreover, integrating new findings into practice poses another challenge, as the landscape of medical knowledge is continuously evolving.

These challenges underline the urgent need for advanced solutions that enhance decision-making capabilities.
